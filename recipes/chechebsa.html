Chechebsa Recipe

Description:

A recipe for Chechebsa (Ethiopian Torn Flatbread Breakfast)! Torn pieces of flatbread are tossed with spiced butter and berbere

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • butter
  • berbere

Steps:

  1. Simply create a batter with flour, salt, and water, then heat in a pan to form individual thin pancake-like flatbread
  2. While still warm, tear the flat bread into pieces and toss with a combination of Niter Kibbeh and Berbere before serving
  3. It can be paired the Chechebsa with plain yogurt, scrambled eggs, a drizzle of honey, and coffee (or tea) for quite the wonderful meal
